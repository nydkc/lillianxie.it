Introduction

My name is Lillian Xie, and I’m currently a junior at Hunter College High School. This year, I’m running to be one of your 2014-2015 Key Club International Trustees. I’ve been part of the K-Family for five years now, and this year is my third as a proud member of Key Club International. As trustee, I aim to improve communication, education, representation, and unification to create a stronger community based on the common values of service and leadership development. This past year, I’ve learned how to communicate with individuals on different levels of the organization, ranging from International officers to club members in my local area. As the Immediate Past Distinguished Lieutenant Governor of Division 11 of NYDKC, I loved being a servant leader and helping the members and officers within my division grow as Key Clubbers and as individuals. In running for International Office, I want to encourage others to share the same passion for service and reach out to individuals across districts to strengthen our organization as a whole. So, trust in me to be your trustee! #LillianforIT
